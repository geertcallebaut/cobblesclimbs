Koppenberg – Flemish Ardennes Climb

Koppenberg is one of the iconic climbs in the Flemish Ardennes, part of Belgium’s legendary cycling landscape.

Distance: 0.56 km
Elevation gain: 61 m
Average gradient: 11.4%
Maximum gradient: 19.6%

This climb is often featured in races like the Tour of Flanders and is part of the rich cobbled road network of the Flemish Ardennes.
