Taaienberg – Flemish Ardennes Climb

Taaienberg is one of the iconic climbs in the Flemish Ardennes, part of Belgium’s legendary cycling landscape.

Distance: 0.64 km
Elevation gain: 30 m
Average gradient: 4.6%
Maximum gradient: 9.2%

This climb is often featured in races like the Tour of Flanders and is part of the rich cobbled road network of the Flemish Ardennes.
