Cobbles & Climbs — Your digital gateway to the raw beauty and legendary spirit of Flemish cycling

Based in the heart of the Flemish Ardennes, we document the region's iconic cobbles and climbs on YouTube.

From the infamous Tour of Flanders sectors to hidden local gems, each video acts as a piece of digital real estate, building a permanent visual archive to preserve its heritage.

Subscribe to support our work and help preserve this unique cycling heritage. Moreover, feel free to join us on Strava, Facebook and Instagram. You are also more than welcome to send us an email – we'd love to hear from you!
