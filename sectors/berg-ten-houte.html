Berg ten Houte – Flemish Ardennes Climb

Berg ten Houte is one of the sectors in the Flemish Ardennes region, part of Flanders legendary cycling landscape.

Distance: 1.1 km
Elevation gain: 65 m
Average gradient: 5.9%
Maximum gradient: 12.8%

This climb was or is featured in spring classics and races like the Tour of Flanders and is part of the rich cycling road network of the Flemish Ardennes.
