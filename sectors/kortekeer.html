Kortekeer – Flemish Ardennes Climb

Kortekeer is one of the sectors in the Flemish Ardennes region, part of Flanders legendary cycling landscape.

Distance: 1 km
Elevation gain: 62 m
Average gradient: 6.2%
Maximum gradient: 15.2%

This climb was or is featured in spring classics and races like the Tour of Flanders and is part of the rich cycling road network of the Flemish Ardennes.
