Karnemelkbeek – Flemish Ardennes Climb

Karnemelkbeek is one of the sectors in the Flemish Ardennes region, part of Flanders legendary cycling landscape.

Distance: 1.3 km
Elevation gain: 76 m
Average gradient: 5.6%
Maximum gradient: 12.2%

This climb was or is featured in spring classics and races like the Tour of Flanders and is part of the rich cycling road network of the Flemish Ardennes.
