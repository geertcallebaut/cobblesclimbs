Knokteberg / Côte de Trieu – Flemish Ardennes Climb

Knokteberg / Côte de Trieu is one of the sectors in the Flemish Ardennes region, part of Flanders legendary cycling landscape.

Distance: 1.2 km
Elevation gain: 88 m
Average gradient: 7.5%
Maximum gradient: 11.1%

This climb was or is featured in spring classics and races like the Tour of Flanders and is part of the rich cycling road network of the Flemish Ardennes.
