Paterberg – Flemish Ardennes Climb

Paterberg is one of the sectors in the Flemish Ardennes region, part of Flanders legendary cycling landscape.

Distance: 0.34 km
Elevation gain: 46 m
Average gradient: 13.4%
Maximum gradient: 18.8%

This climb was or is featured in spring classics and races like the Tour of Flanders and is part of the rich cycling road network of the Flemish Ardennes.
