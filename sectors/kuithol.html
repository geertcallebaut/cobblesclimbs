Kuithol – Flemish Ardennes Climb

Subscribe on YouTube

Kuithol is one of the sectors in the Flemish Ardennes region, part of Flanders legendary cycling landscape.

Distance: 0.5 km
Elevation gain: 40 m
Average gradient: 7.5%
Maximum gradient: 10.6%

This climb was or is featured in spring classics and races like the Tour of Flanders and is part of the rich cycling road network of the Flemish Ardennes.
