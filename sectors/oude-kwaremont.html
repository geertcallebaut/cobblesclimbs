Oude Kwaremont – Flemish Ardennes Climb

Oude Kwaremont is one of the sectors in the Flemish Ardennes region, part of Flanders legendary cycling landscape.

Distance: 2.1 km
Elevation gain: 92 m
Average gradient: 4.6%
Maximum gradient: 10%

This climb was or is featured in spring classics and races like the Tour of Flanders and is part of the rich cycling road network of the Flemish Ardennes.
